November 24, 2015
Grantees
Past and present grantees of Winter Cove Foundation include: Recovery Cafe for the support of marginalized people who may suffer addiction, abuse, or homelessness; College Possible to enable lower income students from public schools to attend college at rates equal to or greater than their more affluent peers; Plymouth Housing seeking to reduce homelessness by providing safe, comfortable and longterm housing for disabled people, low wage working people, and struggling veterans; Ashesi University providing world class college education to Africans who stay in Africa to fill leadership positions in business, not-for-profit service organizations, and government; New Israel Fund to help defend and build civic organizations that enhance democracy in Israel or that defend the civil rights of disadvantaged groups in Israel; International Planned Parenthood Foundation to provide pregnancy counseling, birth control, and abortion services to women struggling to manage the wellbeing of their families; FareStart and Food Lifeline to provide food and jobs to people struggling with economic challenges and food insecurity;
